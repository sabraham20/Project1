YouTube's Significance

The significance of YouTube is immense. The platform has allowed for users to connect with each other and share content. But most important is that this breakthrough has bolstered the impact of education. YouTube has enabled people learn a plethora of subjects with the click of a button. Whether it be learning how to replace a flat tire, or even learning how to program websites.

 I think the success around any product is really about subtle insights. You need a great product and a bigger vision to execute against, but it's really those small things that make the big difference. ______Chad Hurley
